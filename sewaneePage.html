Facts about Sewanee:
  1. Sewanee is a liberal arts college located in Sewanee, TN.
  2. Sewanee was established in 1857.
  3. It is affiliated with the Episcopal Church.
  4. Its motto is: "Ecce quan bonum et quam iucundum habitare fratres in unum,"" which is Latin for: "Behold how good and how pleasant it is for brethren to dwell together in unity."
  5. Greek organizations have a large presence. I myself was in the fraternity Delta Kappa Epsilon.
  6. Remember the seal on my first webpage? That is the seal of Sewanee!
